About Coastal Guide

Coastal Guide is your ultimate companion for exploring the breathtaking coastlines of India. Our mission is to provide you with accurate, real-time information to ensure your beach trips are both safe and unforgettable.

From live weather and tide data to safety alerts and activity recommendations, we've got you covered. Plan your trips, discover hidden gems, and stay informed with our comprehensive guide. Whether you're a surfer, a sunbather, or an adventurer, Coastal Guide helps you make the most of every moment by the sea.
